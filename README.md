# Scaling Mountains
In Scaling Mountains, you are a skier trying to make their way through the slopes of a mountain. Scale up your player to trample over obstacles or shrink down squeeze through tight spaces. The further you get the higher your score. 


How to Play:

    Use the WASD keys to move
    Press spacebar to change your size
    Shrink to avoid skilifts
    Grow big to trample snowmen and wolves
    Score points by touching flags when small or passing through gates when you're big

As you progress your skier will gain speed and the mountain will become more treacherous. Survive as long as you can and get the highest score possible.
